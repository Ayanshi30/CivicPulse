India's urban communities battle a silent infrastructure crisis every day. Potholes, burst pipes, broken streetlights,
and overflowing bins cause accidents, disease, and economic loss yet reporting these issues remains
fragmented, untracked, and opaque. Citizens have no visibility. Municipalities have no prioritization. Issues are
duplicated, misrouted, or simply forgotten.
CivicPulse is a dual-input, AI-powered infrastructure monitoring platform that combines IoT hardware sensors
embedded in roads and public assets with citizen-submitted reports feeding both into a unified AI pipeline
that classifies, deduplicates, prioritizes, and maps every issue in real time using Google Maps. Municipalities get
actionable work queues. Citizens get transparency. Communities get results.
Core Platform Features :-
■ Image & Video Reporting
Citizens upload photo/video evidence. AI extracts issue
type, severity and location from visual content.
■ AI Issue Categorization
Gemini AI classifies every input into 7 issue types and
40+ sub-categories with confidence scoring.
■ Community Verification
3 independent reports at same location trigger
community-verified status, boosting priority score.
■ Real-Time Tracking
Citizens see live status: Reported → Verified →
Assigned → In Progress → Resolved with timestamps.
■ Predictive Insights
Seasonal risk forecasting (monsoon, heat) +
aging-infrastructure trends prevent failures before they
occur.
■ Gamification
Civic Points, leaderboards, and ranks (Observer to
Community Hero) drive sustained citizen participation.
■ Impact Dashboards
Ward councillors get monthly AI-written briefings.
Department KPIs and SLA breach rates are public.
■ Smart Deduplication
GPS + AI cross-reference ensures no duplicate tickets.
